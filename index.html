The Transneptunian Planets

Cupido | Hades | Zeus | Kronos | Apollon | Admetos | Vulcanus | Poseidon

The Uranian system of astrology also uses eight additional planets, all discovered in the last century. These are referred to as the Transneptunian planets. They offer up the kind of detail to a birth chart that increases its interpretive depth exponentially. They "turn up the volume" so we can catch an even more illuminating glimpse of individuality.
